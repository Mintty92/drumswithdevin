BIO

I’m a drummer and audio engineer living in Massachusetts. My interest in music began when I first started playing drums at the age of ten, which led me to receive a degree in Sound Recording Technology. A love of all things percussive, my drumming specializes in all popular, metal, and progressive forms of music. With over 20 years of musical experience touring, recording, private and group instruction, I’m excited to offer personal one-on-one lessons in person and online. If you're interested or just want to say hello, then send me a message with the form below!
